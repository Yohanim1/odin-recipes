Crock Pot Beans

Delicious & Easy Crock Pot Beans

Ingredients

  • 3 C. Pinto Beans
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 1 Serrano
  • 9 C. Water
  • 4 TB. Caldo De Pollo
  • 1 tsp. Pepper
  • 2 tsp. Cumin
  • (Optional) 4 TB. Vegtable Oil

Steps

  1. Fill you Crock Pot with 3 cups of beans.
  2. Quarter the onion, mince the serrano and add them both to the Crock Pot.
  3. Add the seasonings as well as the water to the Crock Pot
  4. Put the lid on the Crock Pot and turn the temperature to high
  5. Cook the beans on high for 9 hours.
  6. Remove Excess liquid with a ladel and reserve if you want to refry your beans.
  7. At this point your beans are done and ready to eat. If you want to refry them add 4 TB. Oil, heat in a heavy bottom pan on medium high, add the beans, and smash them until liquidy. Add reserved bean water until you reach your desired texture.
