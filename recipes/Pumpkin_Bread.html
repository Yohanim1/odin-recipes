Pumpkin Bread

Delicious Pumpkin Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 C. Flour
  • 3 C. Sugar
  • 1 1/2 Tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 Tsp. Nutmeg
  • 1/2 Tsp. Salt
  • 2 Tsp. Baking Soda
  • 1 C. Vegtable Oil
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 C. Pumpkin
  • 2/3rd C. Water
  • (Optional) 1 Bag Peanut Butter Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Farenheit
  2. Mix all of the dry ingredients together.
  3. Mix all of the wet ingredients in a separte bowl.
  4. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients. (Optional) Add bag of peanut butter chips if desired at this point.
  5. Lightly oil the pans and then fill to about 2/3rd full with your mixture.
  6. Bake uncovered for 1 hour or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
